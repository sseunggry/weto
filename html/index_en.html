Participation method and rewards

  • Casual games/
    Rewards

    • Earn points while enjoying casual games such as OX Quiz
    • Provide rewards through ranking system, random draw, etc.

  • Watching
    Advertisements

    • Earn points by watching advertisements

  • Select
    Beneficiaries

    • Select desired beneficiaries by watching videos submitted by people in need of help

  • Provide
    Help

    • Provide reward points by voting for people in need of help registered on the platform with accumulated points

Weto token Operation Structure

  • User
    Activities

    Users perform activities such as quizzes, watching advertisements, and challenges within the My Moment platform

  • Reward points are generated based on activities

  • Weto token
    Conversion

    Points generated to users can be converted to Weto tokens and deposited into personal wallets

    AES: Auto
    Exchange
    Structure

    Weto and reward points are managed by AES(Auto Exchange Structure) to balance supply
